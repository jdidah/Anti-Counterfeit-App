Project requirements 

Hardware requirements
1. A laptop with minimum 4GB RAM, processor speed of 2GHz core i5 and 500 GB Hard Disk Drive.

Software requirements
1.	Linux (Ubuntu 16.04)
2.	Hyperledger Composer
3.	Hyperledger Fabric
4.	Browser (Chrome or Mozilla Firefox)

Hyperledger Composer is an extensive, open development toolset for building blockchain business networks that make it simple and fast for business owners and developers to create and develop blockchain applications to solve business problems.
